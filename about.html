how it helps students

  • Access to Teacher Details: Students can easily explore and access detailed profiles of teachers, including their qualifications, research expertise, and contact information. This enables students to make informed decisions when seeking guidance or selecting teachers for specific subjects or research interests.

  • Research Papers: The app provides a vast collection of research papers contributed by teachers. Students can utilize these resources to enhance their understanding of specific topics, stay updated with the latest developments, and explore areas of interest within their field of study.

  • Ongoing Research Projects: The app showcases ongoing research projects undertaken by teachers. Students can gain insights into cutting-edge research areas and potentially collaborate with teachers on research initiatives, allowing them to actively participate and contribute to the academic community.
